The staff of the second season of The Quintessential Quintuplets revealed in March 2021 that the production on a sequel had begun.[10] The sequel was confirmed to be a film in April 2021.[11] It would serve as the finale for the whole anime series.[12] Pony Canyon was revealed as the film's distributor in October 2021. That month, Yoshitsugu Matsuoka, Kana Hanazawa, Ayana Taketatsu, Miku Itō, Ayane Sakura, and Inori Minase were set to be reprising their respective voice roles as Futaro Uesugi and the Nakano quintuplets Ichika, Nino, Miku, Yotsuba, and Itsuki from the anime series.[13] In December 2021, Masato Jinbo was revealed as the director, Keiichirō Ōchi as the screenwriter, Masato Katsumata as the character designer, Akihito Ougiyama as the art director, Aiko Matsuyama as the color designer, Daisuke Chiba as the cinematographer, and Mutsumi Takemiya as the editor of the film at Bibury Animation Studios.[14]
